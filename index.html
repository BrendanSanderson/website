Cubrism

View on github

In Cubrism, you have been trapped inside a geometric demension and have to fight your way through mazes of over 10 different types of enemies and bosses that chase, shoot and drop bombs in their attempt to defeat you and stop from escaping. You level up and suit yourself up with armor in order to aid your struggle through 50 different levels. Cubrism is an action packed RPG shooter.

Cubrism was developed in Swift using XCode. It uses the SpriteKit Framwork for its physics engine. The art models were made by myself using photoshop. It also uses an online server to store various variables, so that balancing can be down without a full update. I made the decision to have the game save locally to allow for offline play.

Pickie

View on github

Pickie is an application made for you to be able to find your favorite and only your favorite restaurants without having to search different restaurant names over and over. Simply favorite all of the restaurants you like, and everytime that you open the app, Pickie will instantly search for all of your favorite restaurants. Pickie also knows what restaurants are the most favorited allowing for a better customer experience.

Pickie was developed using Objective-C in Xcode. It also uses storyboard. It uses Google API to search information about restaurants. It also has a parse backend to store a user's favorite restaurants as well as to track the amount of times each restaurant has been favorited.
